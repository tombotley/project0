Nintendo Collection

I am a huge fan of video games, which you’ll already know if you’ve bored yourself with my ‘About Me’ section. In particular I love all things Nintendo, an affair that began around 1990 when I got my first console, the NES. Ever since I have been building my collection and now have around a dozen consoles and hundreds of games.

Although the NES wasn’t my earliest gaming experience (that honour went to Dad’s Sinclair ZX Spectrum!) it was the one that combined the excitement of having my very own system and my introduction to the timeless game design of Nintendo.

My favourite Nintendo console would have to be the Super Nintendo (SNES), which also happens to be home to my favourite Nintendo game Super Mario World 2: Yoshi’s Island. The SNES era was in my opinion the golden age of 2D gaming and pixel art and indeed inspires many indie game developers today who continue to create metroidvania and JRPG games very reminiscent of the time.
